About MID Designs

MID Designs (Majestic Interior Drafting & Designs) is a 3D interior design and technical drafting studio led by Talha. We specialize in 3D interior rendering, millwork / joinery shop drawings, and technical furniture drawings for approval.

Our focus is to bridge the gap between design and construction so what you see in the 3D render can be built exactly on site. We support cabinetmakers, millworkers, architects, interior designers and contractors with clear, accurate documentation prepared to professional standards.

Working remotely from Abu Dhabi, we collaborate with clients in different regions, delivering files in formats that integrate smoothly into your workflows.
